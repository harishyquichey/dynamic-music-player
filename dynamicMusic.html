Analysis note

This version first separates ordinary audio activity from sustained rhythmic activity, then locates the first stable pulse grid. Meter is validated on an eighth-note subdivision grid: for example, 5/8 spans five subdivisions, 4/4 spans eight, and 5/4 spans ten. Candidate meters are checked against downbeat accents, internal grouping, repeated bar shapes, neighboring bars, and four-bar phrase placement. Singular bars are retained only when their local evidence and resulting downbeat alignment are stronger than the surrounding meter. The metronome always derives its position from the audio element's current time, so transport changes force an immediate recalculation.
